About HarvestLink
Government‑backed AI assistant to help farmers decide whether to sell, store, or wait at the moment of harvest, reducing post‑harvest loss and distress sales.
Integrated mandi price view (Agmarknet) Nearby buyers & cold storage (≤ 10 km prioritised) Spoilage‑aware decision rules Vernacular voice + SMS summary
